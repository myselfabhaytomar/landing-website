About Debian

Debian is a free and open-source operating system based on the Linux kernel. It is known for its stability, security, and commitment to free software principles. Debian is a community-driven project that provides a versatile platform for a wide range of applications.

Key Features

  • Stable and reliable
  • Wide range of software packages
  • Security-focused
  • Community-driven development
